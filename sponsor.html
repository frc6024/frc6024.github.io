Why Sponsor Us?

We are seeking financial aid from individuals and companies who believe that engagement in STEM activities such as robotics, science, math competitions, and hands-on experiences will encourage a deeper understanding in students. Without our generous sponsors, we would not have the opportunity to get the most out of this wonderful program. The funds and other assistance provided by people like you help our team purchase the tools and parts we need to build our robots, enter competitions, and, of course, order pizza during build season.

We love our sponsors!
